Travrains Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15th July 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Travrains! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, or any other services provided by Travrains. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details.
  • Booking Information: Details related to your travel bookings (e.g., flight, bus, train, hotel reservations).
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and other payment-related data.
  • Identification Documents: If required for certain services (e.g., Aadhar Card, PAN Card applications).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process bookings, provide customer support, and improve our services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., payment gateways, analytics providers) that have their own privacy policies. Please review their policies before using their services.

Your Rights
